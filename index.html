About Us

Tulivu scents and flames is a brand designed to bring peace and calmness as well as a touch of style to your space. Handmade with naturally existing ingredients which burn with mellow flames and tulivu scents.

Tulivu is a swahili word that means peace, calmness or mellow. A fitting word for our products as they make your space tulivu with their calming scents and mellow flames.
